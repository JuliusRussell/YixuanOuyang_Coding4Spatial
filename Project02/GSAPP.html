Yixuan Ouyang

New York City, U.S. / Guangzhou, China

GSAPP, Columbia University

Yixuan is enrolled in the Urban Planning program at Columbia University's Graduate School of Architecture, Planning and Preservation from 2020-2022, with a research focus concentration on urban analysis. Majors include History and Theory of Planning, Economics for Planners, Planning Methods, GIS, Urban Informatics, Environmental Data Analysis, Quantitative Methods, Exploring Urban Data with Machine Learning, and Urban Planning Studio: the Retail Apocalypse. He is expected to graduate in May 2022, and his thesis will focus on the study of the relationship between urban density and COVID spreading.
